- **Will Purchase**: Yes
- **Type**: #simulation #strategy #casual

> ðŸ•¹ï¸ **Description**: Tactical persona-like
> 
> ðŸ‘ðŸ‘ðŸ‘ **Feedback**: This was surprisingly good. Your 4 characters and demon enemies are sorta like chess pieces. You can move and attack so long as you have AP (action points) remaining.
